Arindam Roy Chowdhury

About Research Work Experience CV

I study uncertainty quantification and decision-making under distribution shift—methods that are principled yet practical.

Current Research

  • Nonparametric Confidence Intervals using the Cheap Bootstrap

    Lightweight bootstrap yielding asymptotically exact CIs for bagged predictors with a small, fixed number of resamples as bag size grows; applicable to Random Forests and bagged SAA.

  • Superiority of Naive Optimization via Stochastic Dominance

    When ERM can outperform DRO/regularization in minimizing worst-case regret under joint data and distributional uncertainty.

  • Estimation of Rare-Event Probabilities using Extreme Value Theory

    Tail probability estimation via GPD as a model-agnostic alternative to importance sampling in rare-event simulation.

  • Fantasy-Sports User Behavior Modeling

    Causal impact of promotions/incentives on user engagement at production scale.

  • Multivariate Flight-Data Modeling
    NASA Langley Research Centre

    Tractable polynomial-feature modeling; internal Python package and MATLAB implementation; estimation with non-normalized statistical models.

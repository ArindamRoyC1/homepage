Research

Hi—thanks for stopping by. I study how to make decisions that hold up when data or environments shift. My work focuses on uncertainty quantification and decision-making under uncertainty, aiming for methods that are principled yet practical.

Ongoing work

  • Nonparametric confidence intervals via the Cheap Bootstrap (with Prof. Henry Lam):
    Asymptotically exact CIs for bagged predictors using a small, fixed number of resamples with growing bag size; applicable to Random Forests and bagged SAA in stochastic optimization. Arindam_CV

  • Superiority of naïve optimization via stochastic dominance (with Prof. Henry Lam):
    When ERM can outperform DRO/regularization in worst-case regret under joint data and distributional uncertainty. Arindam_CV

  • Rare-event probabilities using Extreme Value Theory (with Prof. Henry Lam):
    Estimating tail probabilities using GPD as a model-agnostic alternative to importance sampling. Arindam_CV

  • Fantasy-sports user behavior (with Dream Sports):
    Causal impact of platform actions (promotions/incentives) on engagement at production scale. Arindam_CV

  • Multivariate flight-data modeling (NASA Langley):
    Tractable optimization with polynomial features; internal Python package and MATLAB implementation; estimation with non-normalized statistical models. Arindam_CV
